About MoneySave Jr

Turn your lunch money into a smart plan.
Jr helps you save, spend, and budget like a pro.

Why Plan?

  • Make your money stretch all week
  • Plan before you spend
  • Save up for cool stuff (not just snacks)

What You’ll Learn:

  • Save Smart: Track your weekly savings
  • Spend Wisely: Think before you buy
  • Budget Better: Plan how every rand is used
  • Grow Skills: Build habits that’ll help forever

Who Is It For?

  • Learners who want more control over their cash
  • Parents who want to teach saving early
  • Teachers introducing money skills in class
  • Anyone who wants to stop saying “I’m broke” by Wednesday

With Gratitude to FNB App Academy

MoneySave Jr is an comes from a simple idea that even the smallest savings can lead to big changes, especially when you start young. This vision wouldn’t have come to life without the support, training, and inspiration I received from the FNB App Academy.

Thank you for believing in the potential of young developers. Through your mentorship and the full stack development programme, you've empowered the youth to turn code into creativity and creativity into purpose.

This project is not just a portfolio piece. It’s a love letter to learning, to financial literacy, and to the idea that technology can empower kids to make smarter choices.

To the mentors, coordinators, and every peer who walked this journey: Thank you. This is just the beginning.
